Takeyasu Foundry Mar 1947

The company was established as Takeyasu Foundry Co., Ltd. in Iseaski-shi, Gunma Prefecture, and began sand castings business. Later on, renamed to Gunma Gohkin.

GGPC Established Sep 1995

On September 1995, Gunma Gohkin Philippines Corporation was established. Accredited as PEZA Registered business entity.

Commercial Operations Start Jul 1996

Started its commercial operation and 1st shipment to Gunma Gohkin Group & Mitsuba Philippines.

ISO Certification May 2001

Achieved ISO 9001:2000 certification, ensuring compliance with international quality management standards.

Machining Process Added Feb 2003

Started machining process, cutting and processing business started as an added value to the product.

NC ROAD Joins Group Mar 2011

New Creative Road Company Limited (Die-Mold) joined our group.

Factory Expansion Work Fulfilled Aug 2011

Successfully fulfilled factory expansion to support growing operations and capacity.

CDPC joins Gunma Gohkin Group Dec 2014

Creative Diecast Philippines Corporation (Cavite Plant) affiliated with Gunma Gohkin Group

IATF Certified Oct 2018

Achieved IATF 16949:2016 certification, demonstrating excellence in automotive quality management systems.

Product Warehouse & Loading Dock Feb 2023

Completed the construction of a new product warehouse and loading dock to enhance storage and logistics efficiency.

30th Anniversary Sep 2025

Gunma Gohkin Philippines Corporation's 30th Anniversary.
